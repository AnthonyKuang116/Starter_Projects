Sports

During the spring, summer, and autumn seasons I love playing tennis! I started when I joined my high school's varsity tennis team and fell in love with the sport ever since. My senior year of high school my team and I placed 4th in the state of Virginia in 2015. I've kept up with tennis since high school and played it recreationally in college and has become one of my favorite past times.

When it's winter and I can't play tennis, I enjoy skiing. I learned how to ski during my freshman year of college and found the sport really fun! Every year I try to plan a couple ski trips during the winter with some friends.
