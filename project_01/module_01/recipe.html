Simple Recipe for Spicy Tuna Roll

Quick and easy recipe for spicy tuna rolls!

Ingredients

  • 4 oz sashimi-grade tuna
  • 1 1/2 tbsp sriracha sauce
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 green onions/scallions
  • 1 tsp toasted white sesame seeds
  • Seaweed sheets
  • Cooked white rice

Directions

  1. Cut green onions
  2. Combine 1 1/2 Tbsp Sriracha and 1/2 Tsp sesame oil and mix well together
  3. Cut sashimi grade tuna into small cubes, about 1/4 inch pieces
  4. Mix the tuna with sauce and add some green onions
  5. Grab a seaweed sheet and apply thin layer of rice to one side of it
  6. Flip seaweed sheet over and add the mixed tuna and sauce onto the sheet
  7. Roll the seaweed sheet
  8. Garnish with some more green onions and sesame seeds
  9. Enjoy!

Nutrient Facts

Per Serving

196 cal; 28g carbs; 2g fat; 12g protein
